Coursera for Refugees

Refugees worldwide will be able to take more than 1,000 online university courses for free as part of an effort to provide education and skills training under a US-based program launched as part of World Refugee Day.

United States Involvement

The United States is working with the United Nations agencies to increase humanitarian assistance to Syrian refugees, including bridging the education gap for children. Resettlement Agencies are available to provide more information on educational access.
