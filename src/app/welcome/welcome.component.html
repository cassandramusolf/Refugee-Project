Our Mission

To promote world peace and friendship with Syria through providing information, rescoures, and services to Syrian refugees. To help promote a better understanding of the current climate in Syria to Americans through informative action, donations, and volunteer oppurtunities.



This year marks the sixth year anniversy of the start of the civil war. An estimated 11 million Syrians have fled their homes as refugees seeking asylum in other countries. 470,000 people have been killed and another 13.5 million are in need of humanitarian assistance within the country.




Inform

Our focus is to inform the population about the current climate in Syria. By providing the most up to date news articles and tweets about Syria, as well as the differente organizations that help overseas and locally


Connect

We aim to provide Syrian refugees with resources to help them find what they need as quickly as possible. Education, children needs, economics, and health are just some of the topics we focus on.

Donate

Providing our users with the easiest way to donate to multiple organizations. You can check out which agencies provide volunteer help as well as monetary help in Syria and the surrounding regions, and easily donate to the cause!
